# Personal Training Preferences & Constraints

Private notes uploaded by the demo-veteran profile.

## Joint Preferences

- **Right knee:** mild irritation on deep bilateral squats when volume is high.
- Prefer **goblet squat, leg press, or step-ups** whenever the program calls for back squat and the knee feels puffy.
- Avoid max-effort barbell back squats during travel weeks.

## Scheduling Preferences

- Hard sessions best before 8:30am or after 7pm.
- Never schedule legs the morning after a late dinner + drinks social night.
- Hotel weeks: protect sleep over accessory volume.

## Food Preferences (override)

- Default food preference is vegetarian.
- Ok with eggs and dairy; no fish/meat even when traveling.
- Dislikes very spicy meals before morning training.

## Communication Preferences

- Want concrete swaps (“do X instead of Y”), not essays.
- Do not guilt about missed sessions — propose the smallest useful next action.
