# Personal Recipe Collection

Vegetarian-friendly meal ideas uploaded for SteadyFit personal RAG / nutrition tools.

## High-Protein Paneer Bowl

- **Calories:** ~480 kcal
- **Protein:** ~38 g
- **Tags:** vegetarian, under_500, dinner
- **Ingredients:** 120 g paneer, 1 cup cooked quinoa, spinach, tomato salsa, 1 tsp olive oil
- **Notes:** Salt paneer lightly; add chili if you want heat. Fits a ~2100 kcal day leftover after lunch.

## Chickpea Spinach Curry (light)

- **Calories:** ~420 kcal
- **Protein:** ~18 g
- **Tags:** vegetarian, under_500, dinner
- **Ingredients:** 1 cup cooked chickpeas, spinach, onion, tomato, spices, ½ cup cooked rice
- **Notes:** Use spray oil; skip cream. Good busy-weeknight option.

## Greek Yogurt Berry Bowl

- **Calories:** ~310 kcal
- **Protein:** ~28 g
- **Tags:** vegetarian, under_500, breakfast, snack
- **Ingredients:** 250 g nonfat Greek yogurt, mixed berries, 1 tbsp chia, cinnamon
- **Notes:** Post-workout snack if lunch will be late.

## Tofu Veggie Stir-Fry

- **Calories:** ~450 kcal
- **Protein:** ~32 g
- **Tags:** vegetarian, under_500, dinner
- **Ingredients:** 150 g firm tofu, mixed vegetables, 1 tsp sesame oil, garlic, ginger, tamari
- **Notes:** Press tofu 10 minutes; high heat for crisp edges.

## Peanut Butter Overnight Oats (higher cal)

- **Calories:** ~620 kcal
- **Protein:** ~24 g
- **Tags:** vegetarian, over_500, breakfast
- **Ingredients:** 80 g oats, 1 tbsp peanut butter, milk, banana
- **Notes:** Over 500 kcal — not for the “under 500” filter demos.
