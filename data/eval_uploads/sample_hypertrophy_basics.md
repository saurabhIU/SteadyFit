# Hypertrophy Basics (Personal Reference Notes)

Uploaded study notes — not the shared SteadyFit KB. Use for personal-doc RAG evals.

## Weekly Set Targets

| Muscle group | Beginner | Intermediate |
|---|---|---|
| Chest | 8–12 sets/week | 12–18 sets/week |
| Back | 10–14 sets/week | 14–20 sets/week |
| Quads | 8–12 sets/week | 12–18 sets/week |
| Hamstrings / glutes | 8–12 sets/week | 10–16 sets/week |
| Shoulders (side + rear) | 6–10 sets/week | 10–14 sets/week |
| Arms | 6–10 sets/week | 8–12 sets/week |

**Rule of thumb in these notes:** most lifters grow well at **10–20 hard sets per muscle group per week**, split across 2+ sessions.

## Effort and Rest

- Take most hypertrophy sets to **0–2 RIR** (near failure).
- Rest **90–180s** on compounds; **60–90s** on isolation.
- Prefer 6–15 reps for most work; occasional 15–20 for isolation.

## Volume Progression

1. Start at the low end of the range for 2 weeks.
2. Add 1–2 sets to lagging muscles if recovery allows.
3. If joints ache or sleep collapses, cut volume before cutting frequency.

## What These Notes Do Not Cover

These notes do not prescribe medical treatment, meal plans, or supplements. For SteadyFit science framing, prefer the curated KB — these are personal study notes only.
