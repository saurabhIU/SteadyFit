## Physiology of Aging

```yaml
sarcopenia:
  definition: "Age-related loss of muscle mass — 3–5% per decade after 30, accelerating after 60"
  consequence: "Weakness, functional decline, falls"
  solution: "Resistance training is the single most effective intervention (Fiatarone et al., 1994)"

bone_density:
  definition: "Bone mineral density declines with age — osteoporosis risk"
  solution: "Weight-bearing and resistance exercise maintain/improve BMD"
  note: "Swimming and cycling do NOT maintain bone density — must include weight-bearing"

balance:
  risk: "Fall-related injury is leading cause of death in 75+ population"
  solution: "Unilateral exercises, balance training, hip abductor strengthening"

joint_health:
  note: "Moderate exercise reduces osteoarthritis symptoms — rest worsens it"
  contraindicated_if: "Acute joint inflammation/flare; otherwise exercise is therapeutic"
```

---

## Safe Exercise Parameters

```yaml
intensity: "RPE 5–7 out of 10 (moderate — can speak in phrases)"
frequency: "2–3 sessions resistance training per week; 5+ days light activity (walking)"
volume: "Start: 1–2 sets; build to 2–3 sets over 6–8 weeks"
rep_range: "10–15 reps (higher reps = lower load = lower injury risk)"
rest_between_sets: "90–120 seconds (cardiovascular recovery may be slower)"
progression: "5–10% load increase when all reps completed comfortably"
warm_up: "10 minutes mandatory — walking, arm circles, leg swings"
```

## Joint Protection Guidelines

```yaml
knee_osteoarthritis:
  safe: [legs_004, legs_007, legs_013, legs_015, legs_014]
  avoid: [legs_005, legs_001_high_load, legs_009_high_load]
  note: "Exercise reduces OA symptoms; water aerobics also excellent"

hip_replacement:
  post_6_weeks: "Cleared by physio required"
  avoid: [legs_005, deep_hip_flexion_beyond_90_degrees]
  safe_after_clearance: [legs_013, legs_016, legs_007]

shoulder_arthritis:
  safe: [shoulders_008, back_012, chest_014, core_001]
  modify: "Reduce range of motion; avoid overhead if painful"

lower_back:
  safe: [core_003, core_002, core_001, back_014, legs_013]
  avoid_if_acute: [back_001, back_009, core_007]
```

## Balance Training Progressions

```yaml
level_1: "Two feet, eyes open, near wall"
level_2: "Two feet, eyes closed"
level_3: "Single leg stance, eyes open, near wall"
level_4: "Single leg stance, eyes open, arms out — no wall"
level_5: "Single leg stance, eyes closed (near wall for safety)"
level_6: "Single leg + task (head turn, ball toss)"
time_per_level: "30–60 seconds; progress when stable"
frequency: "Daily — balance improves with consistent practice"
```

## Walking Programme (Foundation)

```yaml
week_1_2: "10–15 min/day at comfortable pace"
week_3_4: "20 min/day; include 2 min brisker pace every 5 min"
week_5_8: "30 min/day; aim for 7,000 steps/day total"
ongoing: "8,000–10,000 steps/day associated with significantly reduced mortality (Paluch et al., 2022)"
terrain: "Flat initially; gentle incline as confidence builds"
footwear: "Supportive shoes mandatory; slip-on shoes are a falls risk"
```

## Red Flags — Stop Exercise and Seek Medical Advice
```yaml
stop_immediately:
  - Chest pain or tightness
  - Dizziness or lightheadedness
  - Unusual shortness of breath
  - Sudden joint pain (different to usual muscle soreness)
  - Heart palpitations
```
