# Guide: Obesity (BMI 30+)
Tags: `obesity` `high-bmi` `low-impact` `joint-protection`

## Overview
```yaml
population: "Adults with BMI 30+ or clinically significant excess body weight"
primary_risks: [joint_stress, cardiovascular_load, heat_management, low_fitness_baseline]
primary_goals: [sustainable_fat_loss, cardiovascular_fitness, mobility, confidence]
key_principle: "Non-scale victories matter: fitness, energy, sleep, joint pain reduction"
medical_clearance: "Recommended if BMI > 35 or any cardiovascular/metabolic conditions"
```

## Safe Starting Points

```yaml
preferred_modalities:
  - Swimming (zero joint impact; full body)
  - Walking (low-impact; highly scalable)
  - Cycling (stationary or outdoor; low joint stress)
  - Water aerobics
  - Resistance machines (guided range, reduced fall risk)
  - Chair-based exercises for very deconditioned

avoid_initially:
  - Running (high knee/ankle joint load at high body weight)
  - High-impact HIIT
  - Jump training
  - Standing barbell exercises without established balance and form
```

## Starting Exercise Parameters
```yaml
frequency: "3–5 days/week; include 2 resistance sessions"
cardio_start: "10–15 min walking/swimming; build 5 min/week"
resistance_start: "1–2 sets; 12–20 reps; machines preferred over free weights"
intensity: "RPE 4–6 (light to moderate — sustainable)"
priority_exercises: [legs_015, legs_013, chest_007, back_011, chest_014, core_001]
```

## Calorie Deficit Guidance
```yaml
safe_deficit: "400–600 kcal/day (0.5–0.75 kg/week loss)"
protein_priority: "2.0–2.4 g/kg current body weight (or per kg goal weight for very high BMI)"
why_protein: "Preserves muscle during weight loss; highest satiety of macronutrients"
avoid: "Very low calorie diets (<1200 kcal women, <1500 kcal men) — muscle loss accelerates"
note: "This is general guidance — always defer to registered dietitian for medical weight loss"
```

## Joint Protection
```yaml
knee_protection:
  guidelines:
    - "Avoid impact on hard surfaces initially"
    - "Swim or cycle before progressing to walking, then jogging"
    - "Strengthen quads and glutes before loading knee joint"
  exercises: [legs_015, legs_013, legs_004, legs_007]

lower_back:
  guidelines:
    - "Core stability before loaded spinal exercises"
    - "Avoid deadlifts and heavy rows until form is established"
  exercises: [core_001, core_002, core_003, back_014]
```

# Guide: Type 2 Diabetes
Tags: `diabetes` `type2` `blood-glucose` `metabolic-health`

## Overview
```yaml
population: "Adults with Type 2 Diabetes or prediabetes"
primary_risks: [hypoglycaemia_during_exercise, foot_complications, cardiovascular_risk]
primary_goals: [blood_glucose_management, insulin_sensitivity, cardiovascular_fitness, weight_management]
key_principle: "Exercise is medicine. Both aerobic and resistance training independently improve glycaemic control."
medical_clearance: "Required — especially for those on insulin or sulphonylureas (hypoglycaemia risk)"
```

## Exercise and Blood Glucose

```yaml
acute_effects:
  aerobic_exercise: "Lowers blood glucose during and after exercise"
  resistance_training: "May temporarily raise BG then lower over 24–48h"
  recommendation: "Monitor BG before and after exercise initially"

hypoglycaemia_precautions:
  check_bg_before: "If <5 mmol/L (90 mg/dL), eat 15–20g fast carb before exercise"
  carry_fast_carbs: "Glucose tablets or fruit juice during exercise"
  avoid_exercise_if: "BG >16 mmol/L AND ketones present"

optimal_timing:
  post_meal: "Exercise 1–2 hours after a meal for best glycaemic control"
```

## Recommended Exercise Protocol
```yaml
aerobic:
  type: [walking, cycling, swimming]
  frequency: "5 days/week minimum"
  duration: "30+ minutes"
  intensity: "Moderate (can speak in sentences)"
  evidence: "150 min/week moderate aerobic reduces HbA1c by ~0.6% (Umpierre et al., 2011)"

resistance:
  frequency: "2–3 sessions/week"
  protocol: "2–3 sets × 10–15 reps; all major muscle groups"
  evidence: "Resistance training independently reduces HbA1c; combined training superior to either alone"
  priority_exercises: [legs_007, legs_002, chest_004, back_010, shoulders_002, core_001]
```

## Foot Care (Critical)
```yaml
neuropathy_risk:
  check_feet: "Before and after every session — inspect for blisters, cuts, pressure areas"
  footwear: "Proper athletic shoes; no barefoot training if peripheral neuropathy present"
  avoid: "High-impact activities if severe neuropathy or foot ulcer present"
  exercise_alternative_if_foot_issues: "Swimming or seated exercises"
```

## Nutrition Timing for Diabetics
```yaml
pre_workout:
  if_well_controlled: "Small carb + protein snack 30–60 min before"
  example: "Banana + handful of nuts"

post_workout:
  protein_priority: "Protein within 2 hours to support muscle repair without BG spike"
  example: "Greek yogurt, eggs, legumes"

general:
  carb_distribution: "Spread carbs evenly across 3–5 meals vs large single loads"
  glycaemic_index: "Prefer low GI foods: oats, legumes, most fruits, vegetables"
```

# Guide: Office Workers
Tags: `office` `sedentary` `desk` `posture` `movement-breaks`

## Overview
```yaml
population: "Adults in predominantly sedentary desk-based work (6+ hours sitting per day)"
primary_risks: [lower_back_pain, upper_cross_syndrome, hip_flexor_tightness, cardiovascular_risk]
primary_goals: [counteract_sedentary_posture, reduce_lower_back_pain, cardiovascular_fitness]
key_principle: "Exercise does not fully compensate for 8 hours of sitting. Movement breaks are non-negotiable."
```

## The Office Worker's Posture Problems

```yaml
upper_cross_syndrome:
  tight: [upper_trapezius, levator_scapulae, pectorals]
  weak: [deep_neck_flexors, lower_trapezius, serratus_anterior, rhomboids]
  consequence: "Forward head, rounded shoulders, thoracic kyphosis"
  fix_exercises: [shoulders_005, shoulders_008, back_013, back_011, chest_012_stretch]

lower_cross_syndrome:
  tight: [hip_flexors, lumbar_erectors]
  weak: [gluteus_maximus, abdominals]
  consequence: "Anterior pelvic tilt, lower back pain"
  fix_exercises: [legs_012, legs_013, core_001, core_003, hip_flexor_stretch]
```

## Movement Break Protocol (Every 45–60 Minutes)

```yaml
duration: "2–3 minutes"
exercises:
  option_1:
    - "Stand and walk 50 steps"
    - "10 × arm circles each direction"
    - "10 × hip circles"
  option_2:
    - "15 × bodyweight squats"
    - "10 × shoulder band pull-aparts (keep band at desk)"
    - "30s chest doorframe stretch"
  option_3:
    - "10 × glute bridges on floor"
    - "Cat-cow × 10 reps"
    - "10 × neck retraction (chin tuck)"
evidence: "Standing/walking breaks of 2–3 min per hour reduce sedentary-related metabolic risk markers"
```

## Desk Setup and Ergonomics
```yaml
monitor_height: "Eye level — prevents forward head posture"
chair_height: "Feet flat, knees at 90°"
keyboard_position: "Elbows at 90°; shoulders relaxed"
standing_desk: "If available, alternate 30 min sitting / 30 min standing"
```

## Priority Training for Office Workers
```yaml
frequency: "3 sessions/week minimum"
priority_1: "Posterior chain: glutes, hamstrings, upper back — counteracts sitting"
priority_2: "Core stability: anti-extension and anti-rotation patterns"
priority_3: "Cardiovascular: 150 min/week minimum"

best_exercises:
  - legs_012 # Hip thrust — glute activation
  - back_011 # Chest-supported row — upper back without lower back load
  - shoulders_005 # Face pull — rotator cuff and rear delt
  - shoulders_008 # Band pull-apart — daily at desk
  - core_001 # Plank
  - core_003 # Bird dog — lower back health
  - legs_002 # Goblet squat — hip mobility + glute

avoid_programming_too_much:
  - Chest and anterior deltoid (already overdeveloped from desk posture)
  - Hip flexor strengthening (already shortened from sitting)
```

## Lunch Break Workout (30 Minutes)
```yaml
warm_up_5min:
  - Walking or light jog
  - Arm swings, hip circles

workout_20min:
  - Hip Thrust × 3 × 12
  - Chest-Supported Row × 3 × 12
  - Goblet Squat × 3 × 12
  - Face Pull × 3 × 15
  - Plank × 3 × 40s

cool_down_5min:
  - Hip flexor stretch (kneeling lunge)
  - Doorframe chest stretch
  - Cat-cow × 10
```
