## Program overview

Goal: Lose fat while keeping strength. Training age: 2 years.
Equipment: Full gym Mon/Tue/Wed/Thu/Fri/Sat; hotel gym or bodyweight when traveling.
Sessions per week: 5-6. Session length target: 45-55 minutes.

## Weekly split (default)

### Monday — Upper push
- Bench press or dumbbell press: 3 x 6-10
- Incline dumbbell press: 3 x 8-12
- Cable fly or push-up variation: 2 x 12-15
- Triceps pressdown: 2 x 10-15
- Optional: 10 min incline walk cooldown

### Wednesday — Lower body
- Romanian deadlift or leg curl: 3 x 8-12
- Goblet squat or leg press: 3 x 8-12
- Walking lunges: 2 x 10/leg
- Calf raises: 3 x 12-15
- Core: plank 3 x 30-45 sec

### Friday — Full body + conditioning
- Pull-ups or lat pulldown: 3 x 6-10
- Row variation: 3 x 8-12
- Shoulder press: 2 x 8-12
- Kettlebell swing or bike: 4 x 30 sec hard / 60 sec easy

### Saturday (optional) — Light cardio
- 20-30 min brisk walk or easy cycle. RPE 4-5/10 only.

## Deload week rules

Run a deload every 4-6 weeks, or when sleep is poor for 3+ nights or performance drops 2 sessions in a row.

Deload week changes:
- Cut sets by 40-50% (e.g. 3 sets become 2 sets)
- Keep loads at 80-85% of normal working weight OR same load, stop 2 reps short of failure
- No new exercises; no AMRAP sets
- Keep protein at 1.6-2.0 g/kg; calories at maintenance, not a aggressive cut
- One optional 20 min easy walk only — skip hard conditioning

Example deload week:
- Mon: Upper push (2 sets each exercise, RPE 6)
- Wed: Lower (2 sets each, RPE 6)
- Fri: Full body (2 sets each, skip finisher)
- Sat: Off or 15 min walk

## Travel / hotel gym substitutions

When dumbbells only (up to 40 lb) or limited machines:
- Bench press -> dumbbell floor press or push-ups
- Barbell RDL -> single-leg RDL with dumbbells
- Leg press -> goblet squat or split squat
- Pull-ups -> band-assisted pull-ups or inverted rows
- Session cap: 35-40 minutes; prioritize compounds, 2 sets each

## Progression rules

- Add 2.5-5 lb when all sets hit top of rep range with good form
- If you miss a session: move it within the same week if possible; do not double volume the next day
- After 2 missed sessions in one week: drop Friday optional work and keep Mon + Wed only

## Nutrition notes (personal)

## Injuries and constraints

- Mild lower-back sensitivity: avoid heavy conventional deadlifts from floor; prefer RDL with controlled tempo
- No jumping plyometrics when back is tight
- Warm up 8-10 min before every session (bike + hip hinge drill + light sets)
